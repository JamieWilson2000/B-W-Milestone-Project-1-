Studio Monitors

These are considered to be one of the most important elements in your home studio. Costs range from a modest price (€50 - €100) all the way up into the thousands! Of course, one element to take into consideration is the room itself. Its pointless spending €1500 on a set of excellent studio monitors and placing them in a room with no acoustic treatment on the walls at all. Sure, the power might be there, and to your ears they might sound good, but you won't get a clear representation of how your mix will translate on other systems and environments. Experts would advise that a good rule of thumb would be to treat your room first, acoustic panels on the walls, bass traps in the corners, soft floor coverings, all to catch those pesky bouncing frequencies, and then re-evaluate what's left of the budget and purchase the best you can afford with the balance. You can achieve more with a cheap set of monitors and a well treated room than you can with an expensive set and clean reflective walls!
