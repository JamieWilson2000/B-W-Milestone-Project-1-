Pyramids "Foggy".

Track of the month!
a bouncy feel-good song with a catchy rythmic groove carrying the track along.
Pyramids are a two-piece sibling pop act layering Keys, guitars, electronic drums, bass, backing layers and samples together for a unique sound.
Pyramids:"We have a clean pop sound, but one that is often supplied by veins of the sinister, of mischief, darkness, unease and contradiction. All this is delivered with an unbreakable sense of fun. This is because playing live is what we love best."

Beija Flo "One Of Those Things".

a haunting track featuring a beautifully distressed piano.
Impossible to pin down, Beija Flo’s sound is one that encompasses everything from open hearted balladry to acerbic punk.
Tackling everything from love and relationships through to mental and sexual health, she is truly an artist for our times.
Spontaneous and with an undying lust for creative output, it’s impossible to say what she’ll turn her hand to next.
Her most recent single ‘Nudes’ even came with an exhibition at Output Gallery in Liverpool!"

Samuel Colunga "Green"

Simplicity is the order of the day for this new offering!
Samuel Colunga is a singer-songwriter based in San Antonio who has drawn deeply from his personal experiences
to create an expansive and increasingly eclectic library of songs.
Samuel:" This acoustic traipsing effectively accomplishes a sound that is at once melancholic and playful with a lyrical
voice that leaves my listeners second guessing the casual delivery under the weight of my romantic conviction."

Lisa Wright "Tennessee".

tnm goes all country with this great offering from Lisa Wright
With huge support from local BBC Introducing, Lisa was announced as one of their emerging ‘Sound of 2017s’
and subsequently was handpicked by BBC 6 Music to showcase at Latitude Festival.
Taking inspiration from her favourite artists such as Paul Simon,Patty Griffin and Kasey Musgraves, it’s clear from
your first listen that, to Lisa, lyrics mean everything with Inside Nashville describing her songwriting style as:
"intelligent, elegant and beyond her years".
